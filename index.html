Profile

Geospatial data analyst. My skills involve working with climate, geospatial, and survey data. Also, I have experienced working with satellite data for solving enviromental problems.

Professional Experience

During the past two years, I have worked as a freelancer for private enterprises and local authorities in The Caribbean. Thanks to this experience, I am used to working with professionals of varied backgrounds and stakeholders with different priorities.

Examples

  • I conducted a land-use study in a natural park-protected area through remote sensing, determining vegetation indexes for tracking land cover changes, deforestation, and drought. This helped authorities of the Dominican Republic quantify deforestation in the park during the past five years.
  • In the Caribbean Coast of Colombia, I helped monitor water quality (total suspended solids and chlorophyll-a) by processing satellite images. This allowed local authorities to fill the measurement gaps they had because of not being able to sample during COVID lockdowns.
  • Working for the Ministry of Agriculture in the Dominican Republic, I developed a database of meteorological variables (from global climate models), watersheds, stream orders, and soil association.
