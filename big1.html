Case Studie:Ebola

Ebola is a serious disease caused by a virus(CDC, 2023). During the years 2014 to 2016, the African continent was the most affected and had the worst outbreak. Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone were the countries most affected. The World Health Organization made public all data regarding confirmed and probable cases, critical weeks, and data sources. The dataset represents the best estimates of WHO using methodologies for specific indicators (WHO, 2016).Therefore, it is an extensive database of 32067 records. For this reason, it is important to do the data cleaning, detecting and correcting inaccurate records. It will help to analyze the data for each of the countries, compare them, and be able to make important graphs that allow us to understand the problematic.
